FIRST Robotics 2025 Game: Reefscape

In 2025, we're excited to compete in FIRST Robotics' latest challenge called "Reefscape". In this competition, teams design and build robots to help protect coral reef environments. Our robots will work to remove harmful algae and strategically place new coral to restore reef habitats. Each match presents a unique challenge where teams must demonstrate both precise control and efficient automation.
