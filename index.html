What we're about

FIRST Robotics 2025 Game: First AGE

In 2026, we're excited to compete in FIRST Robotics' latest challenge called "First AGE". In this competition, teams design and build robots. Our robots will work to go to the top. Each match presents a unique challenge where teams must demonstrate both precise control and efficient automation.
